[Peanut butter soya cucumber salad](https://www.instagram.com/reel/C-O1TvVx5yc/)

# Ingredients

- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 2 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 splash of soya
- msg
- chilli crunch
- rice wine vinegar
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- sesame seeds
- sesame oil

# Method

- Put everything in a container, then close and shake well to mix.
