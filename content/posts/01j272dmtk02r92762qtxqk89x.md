# Ingredients

- red onions, sliced and packed in a jar
- ½ jar water
- ½ jar vinegar
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- spices to toast

# Method

- Bring the liquid, sugar, and salt to a boil and pour back into the jar. Top with the spices, shake, and refrigerate overnight.
